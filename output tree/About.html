MD.Balt.JHU.L.119c

Contents:

Verse Epitaph of Female Child (Soteris)


Summary of Support:

Rectangular slab of marble rounded at the top and sides, broken at the upper left hand corner.


Height: 19.3 cm

Width: 8.5 cm

Depth: 3.62 cm


Condition: Upper left hand corner has a break (eliminates 2 words and 1 letter in the first line of text). It is darkened by a nail hole (by which the epitaph was originally attached to the wall of the columbaria (Wilson, 1914, 119)) and remaining nail on the middle right side, affecting the ends of lines 2 and 3.


Layout: There is one column and 6 lines, with even lines indented as a poetic convention to demonstrate the meter. While the text is well centered on the titulum, the spacing between lines is irregular (ranging from 2-1 cm) and there is notable space between the last line and the edge of the stone (4 cm, whereas only 2 cm from the top of the stone to the first line). The letters decrease in size down the rows of text.
